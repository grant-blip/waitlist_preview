## OVERALL VISUAL HIERARCHY RECOMMENDATIONS:

### Color Palette (Luxury Executive):
- **Primary:** Sage green #589351 (keep, but use sparingly)
- **Accent 1:** Champagne gold #D4AF37
- **Accent 2:** Rose gold #B76E79
- **Neutral 1:** Warm cream #FAF8F5
- **Neutral 2:** Soft blush #F9F5F2
- **Neutral 3:** Warm taupe #C9B8A8
- **Dark:** Charcoal #2C2C2C (not pure black)

### Typography Hierarchy:
- **Headlines:** Elegant serif (Playfair Display, Cormorant, or similar)
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Montserrat)
- **Accents:** Thin serif for quotes/callouts

### Imagery Style Guide:
- **Photography:** Editorial quality, natural light, soft focus
- **Color tones:** Warm neutrals (cream, beige, soft sage, blush)
- **Subjects:** Aspirational but relatable—elegant spaces, natural textures, botanical elements
- **Avoid:** Bright colors, clinical/medical imagery, obvious stock photos
- **Embrace:** Soft bokeh, watercolor effects, gold accents, botanical line art

### Spacing & Layout:
- **Generous white space:** Let content breathe
- **Asymmetrical layouts:** More interesting than centered blocks
- **Layering:** Use subtle shadows, overlays, and depth
- **Borders:** Thin gold lines (1-2px) instead of heavy borders
