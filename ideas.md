# Design Ideas for Longevity Reset Waitlist Preview

## Design Style
- **Color Palette**: Earthy, natural tones with a muted green (#699052) for CTAs, complemented by warm neutrals and soft whites
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif fonts for readability and professionalism
- **Layout**: Hero-driven landing page with a strong visual background image, sticky header, and clear vertical flow
- **Art Style**: Natural, health-focused imagery with a serene, aspirational feel

## Key Features
1. **Sticky Header**: Contains event details and a prominent "Join the Waitlist" CTA button
2. **Hero Section**: Large background image with overlay text addressing pain points, embedded video, and waitlist form
3. **Simplified Form**: Only collects Name and Email for maximum conversion
4. **Multiple CTAs**: Strategic placement of "Join the Waitlist" buttons throughout the page
5. **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach ensuring accessibility on all devices

## Brand Alignment
- Maintains the natural, health-focused aesthetic of the original site
- Uses earthy green for CTAs to align with longevity/wellness theme
- Professional yet approachable tone throughout
